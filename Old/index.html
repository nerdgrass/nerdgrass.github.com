About

Hi! I’m Alex. I’m a designer and a developer, focused on making meaningful experiences for users on the web. You can find my code on GitHub, pixels on Dribbble, and finished work below.

I live and work somewhere between artist and engineer. I strive to find the beauty in efficient design, whether that design is ink on paper or code executed on a server. I primarily work with Ruby on Rails and Node.js, though I’ve dabbled with other frameworks and languages.

I currently a proud student of the Startup Institute, furthering my knowledge of web development and attending entirely too many networking events.
